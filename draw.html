Steps for Using Akshyar

For Drawing Canvas:

  1. Click the "Draw Canvas" option below the input container and draw the Devnagari alphabet or word in the white space shown.
  2. After finishing the drawing, click on the "Submit" button for processing and getting OCR'ed output.

For Capturing:

  1. Click on the "Capture" button, which allows the web app to open the webcam/camera present in the device.
  2. Then capture the Devnagari written text, and after capturing, click on the "Submit" button for processing and getting OCR'ed output.
